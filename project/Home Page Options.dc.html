Same deal, two playbooks

What changes when you cross the bridge

日本 · In Japan

Consensus is built before the meeting. Nemawashi means every stakeholder is aligned one-on-one first.

Trust is earned over years. The relationship outlives any single contract — buyers invest in the person.

Silence is information. A pause isn't hesitation — it's respect, and often the real answer.

West · In Australia

The meeting is the decision. Come with the case, expect pushback, leave with an answer.

Trust is earned per quarter. Outcomes and metrics carry the renewal — deliver, then the relationship follows.

Silence is a problem. Speak up early, disagree openly, escalate fast.

I've worked inside both columns — selling to Toyota and local government in Saga, and running 100–400 daily touchpoints for a Melbourne tech operator. That's the bridge.
